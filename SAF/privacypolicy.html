Policy Statement

Sri Asha Forgings Pvt. Ltd. is committed to protecting the privacy of our website visitors, customers, and business partners. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our website or contact us for business enquiries.

01

Information We Collect

We may collect personal and business information such as your name, contact number, email address, company name, location, and technical requirements when you submit an enquiry through our website or contact us directly.

02

How We Use Your Information

The information provided by you is used strictly for business purposes, including responding to enquiries, providing quotations, processing orders, technical discussions, and improving our services. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

03

Information Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal and business information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. All information shared with us is treated as confidential and used only for official business communication.
