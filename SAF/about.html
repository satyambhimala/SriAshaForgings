Our Story:
Forged in Ambition.

Sri Asha Forgings Pvt. Ltd. is a specialised manufacturing company engaged in the production of high-quality aluminium alloy forgings and precision machined components for critical industrial applications. Since its establishment in 2004, the company has built a strong reputation for engineering excellence, reliable quality, and customer-focused manufacturing solutions.

We manufacture a wide range of forged components including forged rings, shafts, rods, flats, plates, discs, and custom forged components as per customer drawings and specifications. Our manufacturing operations combine traditional forging expertise with modern machining and inspection systems.

Philosophy

Zero-defect manufacturing. We don't just shape metal; we engineer reliability into every fibre.
