Materials & Products

We specialize in aluminium alloy forgings including grades such as 2014, 2219, 2618, 5083, 5086, 6061, 6351, 7020, 7075, and Aluminium Lithium alloy 2195.

We manufacture forged rings, forged discs, shafts, rods, flats, plates, blocks, long tube forgings, conical forgings, and custom forged components as per customer drawings and specifications.

Services & Industries

Yes, we provide heat treatment, machining, and finishing services as per customer technical and engineering requirements.

We supply forged components to industries such as aerospace, defense, automotive, heavy engineering, industrial machinery, oil & gas equipment, and power generation.

Yes, we specialize in manufacturing custom forged components based on customer drawings, technical specifications, and material requirements.

Quality & Orders

We conduct ultrasonic testing (UT), dimensional inspection, mechanical testing, and metallurgical inspection to ensure product quality and reliability.

We do not have strict minimum order quantity requirements and can manufacture components based on customer needs and project requirements.
