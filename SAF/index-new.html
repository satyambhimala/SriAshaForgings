Our Story

THE SRI ASHA
FORGING STORY

Sri Asha Forgings Pvt. Ltd. is a specialised manufacturing company engaged in the production of high-quality aluminium alloy forgings and precision machined components for critical industrial applications. Established with a commitment to engineering excellence and consistent quality, the company has built a strong reputation for delivering reliable forged components to demanding industries.

Our core capabilities include open die forging, heat treatment, and precision machining, allowing us to manufacture a wide range of products such as forged rings, shafts, rods, flats, plates, discs, and customised forged components as per customer specifications.

With a strong focus on reliability, innovation, and customer satisfaction, Sri Asha Forgings Pvt. Ltd. proudly serves industries such as aerospace, defence, automotive, heavy engineering, and industrial manufacturing by providing durable and high-performance forged components.

Our Facility

OUR INFRASTRUCTURE

Sri Asha Forgings Pvt. Ltd. operates a well-equipped manufacturing facility designed to produce high-performance aluminium alloy forgings with precision and consistency. Our infrastructure combines robust forging equipment with modern heat treatment and machining capabilities to support the production of complex forged components.

The facility is equipped with heavy-duty open die forging hammers, advanced drop-bottom furnaces for controlled heating and heat treatment, and precision machining systems that allow accurate finishing of forged products. These systems enable us to manufacture forged rings, shafts, rods, plates, discs, and specialised components with excellent dimensional accuracy and metallurgical quality.

Dedicated material handling systems and process control equipment ensure efficient production and safe handling of large forged components. Our facility also includes quality inspection and testing areas where materials are verified through ultrasonic testing, mechanical testing, and metallurgical inspection.

This strong infrastructure allows Sri Asha Forgings to maintain consistent quality standards while meeting the demanding production requirements of industries such as aerospace, defence, heavy engineering, and industrial machinery.
